Track Stock Levels

Our software allows you to track your stock levels in real-time, ensuring that you never run out of essential salon products. You can set alerts to notify you when stock levels fall below a certain threshold, allowing you to reorder products before they run out

Manage Purchase Orders

Our software makes it easy to manage purchase orders, allowing you to track your orders from placement to delivery. You can generate purchase orders directly from the software, and track them until they are delivered and added to your inventory.

Barcode Scanning

Our software supports barcode scanning, making it easy to scan products in and out of your inventory. This feature allows you to quickly and accurately track your stock levels, reducing errors and improving efficiency.

Reporting

Our software provides a range of reports to help you gain insights into your inventory operations. You can generate reports on stock levels, purchase orders, and product usage, helping you make informed decisions about your inventory management.

Product Usage Per Service

Our software allows you to categorize your products, making it easy to find and manage products in your inventory. You can categorize products by brand, type, or any other criteria that makes sense for your salon.
